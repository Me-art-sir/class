This story follows billionaire playboy Oliver Queen, who returns to Star City after being presumed dead in a shipwreck. While stranded on a remote island, Oliver develops survival and combat skills that transform him into a vigilante. Taking on the persona of the "Green Arrow," he uses his skills to fight crime and corruption, uncover family secrets, and protect his city.
